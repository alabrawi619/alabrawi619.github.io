## WHY A TERMINAL THEME?

```bash
$ reason --list-all

[*] Terminals are honest. No gradients. No shadows. Just text and logic.
[*] Monospace fonts make code beautiful.
[*] Green on black is easy on the eyes at 2 AM.
[*] Nostalgia is a valid engineering requirement.
```

There is something deeply satisfying about the constraints of a character-mode display. Every pixel — or in this case, every character cell — must earn its place.
