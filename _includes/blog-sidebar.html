/.md` is grouped under its section (the sub-directory name), so creating a new folder under blogs/ automatically produces a new section. Files placed directly in blogs/ (e.g. index.md) are excluded because their page.dir is exactly "/blogs/". Example: blogs/cloud/aws.md -> section "cloud"; blogs/cyber/nmap.md -> "cyber". {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign blog_docs = site.pages | where_exp: "p", "p.dir contains '/blogs/'" | where_exp: "p", "p.dir != '/blogs/'" -%} {%- assign grouped = blog_docs | group_by: "blog_section" | sort: "name" -%}
